Amy Coney Barrett

Interesting Facts

  • Amy is only 50 years old!
  • Amy is the 5th woman to serve on the Supreme Court.
  • Amy earned her undergraduate degree (magna cum laude) from Rhodes College.
  • Amy earned a juris doctor (summa cum laude) at Notre Dame Law School.
  • Amy was confirmed to the Supreme Court in 2020.
  • She had a long career in law as a distinguished professor and judge.
  • Amy and her husband Jesse Barrett have seven children.
  • Two children were adopted from Haiti and one son was born with Down syndrome.

Amy Coney Barrett is inspiring! She is on the U.S. Supreme Court.
