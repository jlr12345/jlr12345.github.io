Amy Coney Barrett

I am inspired by Amy Coney Barrett, she is currently an Associate Justice on the U.S. Supreme Court.

  • Amy is only 50 years old!
  • Amy is the 5th woman to serve on the Supreme Court.
  • Amy earned her undergraduate degree (magna cum laude) in 1994 from Rhodes College in TN and earned a juris doctor (summa cum laude) at Notre Dame Law School in IN in 1997.
  • Before Amy was confirmed to the Supreme Court on October 26, 2020, she had a long career in law as both a distinguished professor and judge.
  • Amy and her husband Jesse Barrett have seven children--two of whom they adopted from Haiti and a son born with Down syndrome.
